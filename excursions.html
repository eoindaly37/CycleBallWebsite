Training

Our weekly training takes place on Wednesdays. These usually take place from 7pm-9pm, although we are flexible with times. Training for cycle ball is intense. It is a very involving sport where you must be at peak fitness to compete. Training takes place in CIT's Sports Stadium which is located near the Rubicon building.

Trip

Our group trips take place monthly. The destination can vary, although it is the team that have a vote to decide where we will end up! Some of our past trips have been to Berlin, Amsterdam and even Kilcummin! The cost of trips will be included in the membership fee, which will be calculated based on your level of experience, age and occupation.

Final

This is our final that takes place yearly. Two captains are selected which teams are picked from. Winners can look foward to winning fantastic prizes. CIT Cycle Ball is now sponsored by O'Sullivan Cycles which means the prizes are all the more lucrative.
